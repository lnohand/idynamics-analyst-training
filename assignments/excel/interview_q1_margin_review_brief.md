## What You're Building

Open the file: **`iDynamics_Excel08_Q1_Margin_Review.xlsx`**

Read the **Instructions** tab, then read the **Email** tab. David's questions are there.

Use the **Analysis** tab to build your analysis. Add columns, rows, or additional tabs as needed.

Fill in the **Commentary** sections in the Email tab reply — one per question.

| Tab | Contents |
|-----|---------|

| Subscriptions | Q1 subscription-level data — 125 rows across January, February, and March |

## Guidelines

- No formula hints. Build the analysis the way you would on the job.
- Your reply commentary should be written as if you are sending it to David — specific numbers, business language, no filler.
- Each question gets 2-4 sentences. Be direct.
- If you notice anything in the data beyond what David asked, include it.
